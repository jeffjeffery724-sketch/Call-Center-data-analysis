## 📊 Call Center Dashboard
## 🧭 Overview

This dashboard shows how well a call center is performing. It helps you understand customer satisfaction, common issues, and how different channels are doing.

## 🔑 Key Numbers
⭐ CSAT Score (5.33): How happy customers are overall
⏱️ Call Duration (26.26): Average time spent on calls
😊 Positive Sentiment (10): Number of positive customer interactions
## 📌 What You Can See
🏙️ City by Reason: 
Shows why customers are contacting support (billing, payments, outages)  
🧠 Sentiments:
Shows if customers feel positive, neutral, or negative  
📍 CSAT by Call Center:
Compares customer satisfaction across different locations  
📡 CSAT by Channel:
Shows which channel performs best (call, chatbot, web, email)  
🗺️ State by Reason:
Shows which states have more customer issues  
💬 Sentiments by Channel:
Compares customer feelings across channels  
## 🎛️ Filter
You can filter data by:
Billing
Payments
Service Outage
## 💡 Why It’s Useful
Find common customer problems
See which channels work best
Improve customer service
## 🏁 Summary

This dashboard helps you quickly understand customer experience and improve support services 🚀
