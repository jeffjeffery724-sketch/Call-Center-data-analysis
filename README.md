## 📊 Call Center Performance Dashboard
## 🧭 Overview

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of call center performance, customer satisfaction, and sentiment trends across multiple channels, locations, and issue types. It helps stakeholders quickly identify operational strengths 💪, customer pain points ⚠️, and opportunities for improvement 📈.

## 🔑 Key Metrics
⭐ Average CSAT Score (5.33):
Measures overall customer satisfaction across all interactions.
⏱️ Average Call Duration (26.26):
Indicates the typical length of customer calls, helping assess efficiency.
😊 Positive Sentiment Count (10):
Tracks the number of interactions classified as positive.
## 🏙️ City by Reason
Displays the volume of issues categorized by reason:
💳 Billing Questions (highest volume)
💵 Payments
⚡ Service Outages
Helps identify the most common customer concerns.
## 🧠 Sentiments Distribution
Pie chart showing the proportion of:
😊 Positive
😐 Neutral
😠 Negative interactions
Provides a quick snapshot of overall customer mood.
## 📍 CSAT by Call Center
Line chart comparing customer satisfaction across call center locations:
Los Angeles
Baltimore
Chicago
Denver
Useful for benchmarking performance between centers 📊.
## 📡 CSAT by Channel
Shows satisfaction scores across communication channels:
📞 Call Center (highest)
🤖 Chatbot
🌐 Web
📧 Email (lowest)
Helps evaluate channel effectiveness.
## 🗺️ State by Reason
Breaks down issue frequency by state.
Highlights geographic trends in customer concerns.
## 💬 Sentiments by Channel
Compares sentiment volume across:
📞 Call Center
🤖 Chatbot
🌐 Web
📧 Email
Identifies which channels generate more positive or negative experiences.
## 🎛️ Filters
Reason Filter:
💳 Billing Question
💵 Payments
⚡ Service Outage
Allows users to drill down into specific issue types for deeper analysis 🔍.
💡 Insights & Use Cases
Identify high-volume issue categories to prioritize improvements 🛠️.
Compare performance across call centers and channels 📊.
Monitor customer sentiment trends over time 📉📈.
Optimize resource allocation and customer support strategies ⚙️.
🏁 Conclusion

This dashboard enables data-driven decision-making by combining customer satisfaction metrics, sentiment analysis, and operational insights into one powerful view 🚀.
